CUTTIN' CLASS XX - April 24, 2004
Our 20th Anniversary concert in Statler Auditorium was a grand celebration featuring the Class Notes of 1983-2004. We invited our alumni back to sing with one alumni group representing the Class Notes from '83-'92 and the other representing the '93-'01 Class Notes.
