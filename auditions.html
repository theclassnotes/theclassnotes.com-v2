Come be a part of Cornell's original coed a cappella group! ALL VOICE PARTS ARE WELCOME! Auditions are held at the beginning of each semester and consist of a few scales, some pitch-matching, and a short solo performance. They're quick, laidback, and of course, FUN! We would love to hear you, so be sure to stop by! All we ask is that you please prepare a short, contemporary pop solo. We look forward to meeting you!
